Site Name & Purpose

Bountiful Baseball - This name represents a little league baseball organization in the city of Bountiful.

Purpose

This site will provide information about the organization, its teams, and contact information for inquiries. There will be a sign-up for interested players. The site will also provide a teams page with all relevant information, including team rosters, schedules, and standings.

Scenarios

1. A parent wishes to sign their child up for the baseball league, and later has questions about the game schedule, including coach information or administrative contacts.

2. What team is my child on and who is the coach?

3. Where can I see the practice schedule?

Color Scheme

The color scheme I am going to try to make work for this site will include the following colors:

  • Red (--madder: #a31621ff)

    This will be used for the primary color.

  • --baby-powder: #fdfffcff

    This will be used for the background.

  • --slate-gray: #6d8a96ff

    This will be used for borders and contrast accents.

  • --eerie-black: #1b1b1eff

    This will be used for text.

  • --moss-green: #96a13aff

    This will be used for success messages and other elements that require a positive emphasis.

Typography

This site will use Montserrat bold 700 for headers and Work Sans for body text.
